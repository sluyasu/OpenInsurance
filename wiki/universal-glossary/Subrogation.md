## TL;DR

Subrogation is the insurer's right, after paying a claim, to step into the policyholder's shoes and recover the
amount from the party responsible for the loss.

## Details

Subrogation prevents double recovery and shifts the cost back to the at-fault party. Policies often describe the
insurer's recourse rights and any cases where recourse against the insured is possible (for example after an
excluded circumstance).
