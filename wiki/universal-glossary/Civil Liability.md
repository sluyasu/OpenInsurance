## TL;DR

Civil liability is the legal obligation to compensate a third party for harm one causes. Liability insurance
transfers the financial consequences of that obligation to the insurer, within the limits of the policy.

## Details

Most mandatory or near-mandatory covers are liability covers: motor third-party liability ([[Auto]]) and
private/family liability ([[Vie privée]]). Liability insurance pays the third party, not the policyholder, and
typically excludes intentional acts.
