- Patronale Life SA — Belgian life insurer (broker-distributed) + mortgage lender. 15 products enumerated across Tak 21 (7), Tak 23 (5), Tak 26 (3). 14/15 resolved with real document URLs; only "Huurwaarborg en Kapitalisatie" left with url="?" (see below).
- Doc types: pal_eid = essentieel informatiedocument (PRIIPs KID); pal_nif = financial info fiche (FIF, older "financiële infofiche") — both bucketed as doc_type=kid (no FIF enum); pal_avo / av_* = algemene voorwaarden (conditions générales); pal_sid = fund-specific KID; pal_reg / beheersreglement = fund management rules (bucketed conditions_speciales).
- Edition tokens decoded: 4-digit = YYMM (2601=2026-01, 2602=2026-02, 2603=2026-03, 2501=2025-01); 3-digit = YY+version (251 = 2025 v1, 231 = 2023 v1). Editions left "?" where the filename has no token (financiele_infofiche_pla, beheersreglement_pla, pal_fif_uvv_nl).
- SHARED CG: pal_avo_251_nl_sbv.pdf (sbv = spaarverzekering / savings-insurance general terms) is the single conditions générales linked from all Tak 21 savings products AND the Tak 23/26 investment/capitalisation products — listed per product because it is that product's CG.
- Schuldsaldoverzekering (outstanding-balance / mortgage-protection life, branch tagged autres) is a family with sub-products (hypo-secure, hyposelect, select, short-term-cover at /nl/levensverzekeringen/tak-21/schuldsaldoverzekering/<sub>). The parent page exposes only 2 CG docs (Select = pat_avo_251_nl_sel, Short Term Cover = pal_avo_231_nl_stc); per-sub-product KIDs live on the 4 sub-pages (not individually fetched).
- Family Care bundles a death-capital + funeral (UVV = uitvaartverzekering) component → tagged obseques; has its own KID (cdep), FIF (cdep), a funeral FIF (uvv) and dedicated CG (av_family_care).
- Private Life Account uses the older doc set (financiele_infofiche_pla FIF + beheersreglement_pla) rather than a pal_eid KID.
- Patronale Life Flexible: distributed only via broker Octavio (octavio.be); adds a fund-specific SID + beheersreglement for the PLSCS60 internal fund.
- Fork Account (Tak 26 capitalisation): 6 separate KIDs, one per formula (fa1, fa2, fa3, fa5, fa7, fax) — same path with the suffix swapped; only fa1 listed as the representative URL.
- UNRESOLVED — Huurwaarborg en Kapitalisatie (Tak 26 rental-guarantee): commercialised and distributed EXCLUSIVELY by partner Korfine (korfine.be, info@korfine.be); no product PDF is hosted on the Patronale site, so url="?" (docs would be on korfine.be).
