notes: CG are AXA-rebranded, NOT own. The product names ("Confort Auto", "Confort Habitation Flex", "Confort Vie Privée", "Confort Personnes", "Confort Spéciale Habitation") and the numeric document references (4187xxx / 4186xxx) are AXA Belgium's catalogue; the site itself states the brand is "adossée à l'un des plus grands groupes d'assurances mondial" (= AXA). L'Ardenne Prévoyante is a regional AXA distribution brand and the risk carrier is AXA Belgium SA. Underlying risk carrier: AXA Belgium SA.
Scope of this file: the library is very large (~440 PDF links across four tabs = current CG + IPID + "anciennes"/superseded editions + NL duplicates like "Comfort Auto – Aansprakelijkheid"). This table enumerates the CURRENT consumer product lines (auto incl. camion, habitation/incendie, vie-privée/RC familiale, accidents, chasse, associations) plus the head professional families (cyber, accidents du travail, RC exploitation, RC professionnelle) with their latest "en vigueur au" edition. NOT exhaustively listed here (see library URL for full set): each CG has a paired IPID; a full professionnels catalogue exists (pertes d'exploitation, incendie/vol risques simples & spéciaux, RC après livraison, RC (para)médical/agents immobiliers/vétérinaires/experts-comptables/bureaux d'études, TRC tous risques chantier, Global Truck, package "assurances entreprises" with Lexique/Assistance/Dispositions administratives sub-docs); NL-language versions; and "ANCIENNES CG" tabs holding superseded editions. Edition = latest date shown in the library table (some re-issued 04-13/02/2026); underlying PDF may carry an older internal date. A dedicated IPID for each product is downloadable from the 4th column of the same table.
