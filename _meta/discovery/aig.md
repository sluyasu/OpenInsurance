notes: PUBLIC IPID library CONFIRMED and fully public at https://www.aig.be/ipid (no login). AIG is a broker-distributed commercial/specialty insurer, but it publishes an open IPID library. Products are all commercial/specialty lines (financial lines, liability, accident & health, travel) — no auto/habitation retail. Each product is published in FR, NL and EN; only the Belgian-market current editions are enumerated above (FR+NL; EN also available for every product, EN-only for FI Crime/FIDO/FIPI/Fund Protector/IMI/PE Protector). A parallel set of Luxembourg-market IPIDs (Casualty/General Liability, Product Contamination, plus LUX A&H duplicates) also lives on the same page but is out of scope for the Belgian market. No conditions_generales PDFs are published openly — only IPIDs; full general conditions are provided at contract via the broker.
