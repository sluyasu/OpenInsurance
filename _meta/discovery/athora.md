The 24 CG (conditions_generales) rows above are the full current set of life-product general conditions; several product names recur across codes (e.g. Self Life Dynamico = 1F758/1F806/1F891) = successive editions/variants of the same product. Only Safe Horizon 3/5 expose an explicit edition (ED0924 = 2024-09); the other CG filenames carry no edition token. Legacy Generali-era products (Crescendo, Ventisei) are closed to new business but their CG stay published. BEYOND these CG, the library holds ~470 supporting financial documents I did NOT enumerate (representative KID/DIC included above): ~180 DIS-FR-* (Documents d'Informations Spécifiques, per product×fund), ~40 FAC-FR-* (fiches fonds), ~40 REG-FR-* (règlements de gestion des fonds), ~80 SFDR annexes, ~10 top-level DIC-FR-* PRIIPs KID, plus a few INFO_FICHE.
