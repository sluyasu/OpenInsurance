notes: The ONLY policy documents Crelan self-hosts publicly are the Visa Classic / Visa Gold credit-card insurance packages — underwritten by Inter Partner Assistance SA (AXA group; claims via axa-assistance.com). Current editions dated 01/07/2026; "jusqu'au 30/06/2026" versions exist and are superseded. Older CG for both cards: https://www.crelan.be/sites/default/files/documents/Conditions-generales-Visa-Classic_jusqu-au.pdf and .../Conditions-generales-Visa-Gold_jusqu-au.pdf (superseded).
Crelan's OWN vs AXA-agent split: Crelan SA (FSMA 0205764318) is a **tied agent of AXA Belgium SA for ALL non-life branches AND for the solde restant dû**. The "assurance solde restant dû" is an AXA Belgium branch-21 life product (not Crelan-underwritten); its CG + fiche produit live on axa.be, not crelan.be. Likewise non-life (auto/véhicules, habitation-incendie, familiale, vélo, voyage, students travel, biens & bénéfices, RC) are AXA products distributed by Crelan as agent → documents on axa.be. Net: Crelan has no self-underwritten consumer product library beyond the co-branded Visa cards. NL equivalents of all pages exist (crelan.be/nl/...).
