notes: B2Bike is a bicycle-leasing provider, not an insurer. Its "verzekering" component (omnium: theft + accidental damage, plus 24/7 roadside assistance) is bundled inside the lease package rather than sold as a standalone policy. No public policy-document library on b2bike.be: the homepage (scrape) shows only marketing, a firecrawl_map (search "verzekering voorwaarden conditions assurance legal documenten") returned only the homepage, and a web search surfaced only the FAQ (b2bike.be/en/faqs — gives a roadside-assistance phone number, no CG/IPID). The underwriting risk carrier is not disclosed on the public site; the insurance terms are delivered with each individual lease contract. If catalogued later, branch = velo.
