Both a firecrawl_map (search: "arbeidsongevallen algemene voorwaarden IPID") and a domain-restricted firecrawl_search returned only prevention/training/expertise pages — no policy-wording or IPID PDFs. Mensura operates primarily as an External Service for Prevention and Protection at Work (EDPBW/SEPP) and runs a statutory workplace-accident insurer, "Mensura Gemeenschappelijke Verzekeringskas tegen Arbeidsongevallen" (Caisse Commune d'Assurance contre les Accidents du Travail) — a mutual insurance fund that is itself the risk carrier. Belgian accidents-du-travail cover is governed by the law of 10/04/1971; Mensura distributes its arbeidsongevallen policy via quote/broker and does not publish the algemene voorwaarden or IPID online (unlike Belfius, Vivium, Allianz, Securex, whose AT algemene voorwaarden ARE public). Branch would be accidents-travail if/when docs surface. Site primary language NL (FR version at mensura.be/fr/...).
