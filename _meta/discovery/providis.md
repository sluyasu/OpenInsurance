notes: Brand of AG Insurance distributed via brokers and via BNP Paribas Fortis. The product page states plainly "L'assurance Protection juridique Providis Protection Juridique Globale est un produit d'assurance d'AG." The CG PDFs are Providis-branded OWN documents (hosted on providis.be, titled "Providis Protection Juridique Globale / Providis Globale Rechtsbijstand"), underwritten by AG — so own-branded, parent carrier = AG. Single global family product with three formules (Start / Classic / Excellence) + optional mobility extensions; one CG per distribution channel (courtier: 00798324700F/N ; BNP Paribas Fortis: 00793333500F, current edition = F2025, superseded = Bank_F_2021). IPID is NOT published as a direct download — page says the IPID is "gratuitement disponible auprès de votre intermédiaire d'assurance". No KID.
