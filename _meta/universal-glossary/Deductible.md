## TL;DR

A deductible (or excess) is the portion of a loss the policyholder bears before the insurer pays. It can be a
fixed amount, a percentage, or a combination.

## Details

Deductibles align incentives and reduce small claims; a higher deductible usually lowers the premium. The Belgian
term is [[Franchise]].
