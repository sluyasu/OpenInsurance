## TL;DR

The premium is the price the policyholder pays for cover, usually annual and often payable in instalments. It is
set by underwriting and may be indexed over time.

## Details

The premium reflects the insured risk, the chosen guarantees, the [[Deductible]], and (for some lines, such as
motor) the claims history. It is distinct from any deductible borne on a claim.
